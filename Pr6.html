MET Bhujbal Knowledge City Nashik

Ritima Chaudhari

MET Bhujbal Knowledge City (BKC) in Nashik, established by the Mumbai Educational Trust (MET) in 2006, is a large, multidisciplinary educational hub offering engineering, management, pharmacy, and other programs, known for its sprawling campus, AICTE/NBA/NAAC approvals, affiliation with SPPU, and focus on developing globally-ready professionals\ through quality, relevant learning systems.
